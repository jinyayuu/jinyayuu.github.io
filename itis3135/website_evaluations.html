Website Evaluations

Ling's Cars

A brightly colored website selling used cars with many moving images. Key colors include bright reds, yellows greens. There's also many with pictures of cars and Ling, and big fonts being used.

  • Contrast: 3/5 - Theres almost too many background and text colors. Font sizing is also varies very widely.
  • Repitition: 1/5 -There is little consistency amoungst fonts, sizing, and colors.
  • Alignment: 1/5 - There is little use of guide lines outside of the navigational bars.
  • Proximity: 3/5 - Elements are spaced evenly together, though there are some spots that have a large gap.

Final Assessment: 8/20pts - Does not follow CRAP principles very well, making the website feel bright and scattered.

Splatoon 3 Official Site

Colorful site that showcases the paint/graffite like aesthetic of Splatoon. Buttons have a paint like animation, along with sizeable media content of the game.

  • Contrast: 5/5 - Usage of a specific color scheme, with important elements being colored ontop of a dark background.
  • Repitition: 5/5 - Consistency in font family, size, accent colors, and button styling.
  • Alignment: 5/5 - Clear horizontal dividing of the page. Makes the scrolling experience pass through distinct sections.
  • Proximity: 5/5 - Text and interactable elements are in proximity to each other. As well as each section's content fiting in the screen.

Final Assessment: 20/20pts - Colorful but consistent site that shows the game's personality but still deliver information concisely.
