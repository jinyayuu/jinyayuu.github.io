Being An Artist

No one artist is ever the same! Each artist has their own distinct style, assortment of tools, and techniques that let transcribe their ideas into the real world.

I personally am a digital artist, who uses a EX-Pen Deco drawing tablet along with my Asus laptop. The digital program I use is Paint Tool SAI for illustration along with Figma for graphic design cases. My illustrative style use to be rather anime in that there was emphasis on line work and cel-shaded coloring. Now I've adopted a more paintery style with colored rendering and painted textures!

About Me

Hello! I'm an artist and a big consumer of anything pretty, dramatic, and story driven, which I hope to reflect in my art! Currently focusing on full rendered illustrations :).

I first started illustration at a very young age, but with more traditional mediums such as acrylic painting, sketching, chinese painting, and many more. I started doing digital illustration when I was given my first Ipad. Later in life I was gifted an old graphics tablet, which really launched me into doing digital art over traditional.

Commissioning

What is a Commission?

I do commissioned work! A commission is a request for an artist to illustrate something for you. Think when Kings would commission paintings for their palace, same idea! Most cases now a days, commissioned work is used for personal or commercial intentions. There are many artists that live off doing commisions for clients,

My Commission TOS

Artist's Rights

  • I am allowed to decline any request for whatever reason I may have.
  • I am allowed to cancel a request if the client is excessively rude or inappropriate. The client will be blacklisted and not refunded.
  • I am allowed to use the art for self-promotion and record the process on social media unless discussed otherwise.
