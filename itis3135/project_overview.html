Project Overview

Project StevenYang.Co

The project will entail developing a website for my client Steven. They are a freelance software engineer and want a website where they can show case their experience, act as a landing page for potential recruiters, and be a point of contact for those looking to hire for freelance work. Thus the intended users of this website will be anyone who is interested in learning more about Steven, as an individual and a programmer.

Content of this website will include a landing page, page of experience with programmed applications, about page of Steven, a contact form, and lastly a app directory.

Page Design

  1. Home
    • Purpose: Landing page that gives an overview of main points of interest.
    • Audience: The casual viewer.
    • Content: Quick general blurbs about Steve, and important links.
    • Contains: Hyperlinks, images.
    • Page Action: Quick link hyperlinks will open in a new tab.
    • User Input: None.
    • Validation: None.
  2. About
    • Purpose: General information about Steven.
    • Audience: Users.
    • Content: General information and frequently asked questions.
    • Contains: Dropdowns.
    • Page Action: FAQ will be collapsed quetions that reveal answers .
    • User Input: None.
    • Validation: None.
  3. Experience
    • Purpose: Show case Steven's programming experience and projects.
    • Audience: Recruiters.
    • Content: Languages learned, current and previous projects, past job roles.
    • Contains: Hyperlinks and buttons.
    • Page Action: Buttons will hide and show coresponding project sections.
    • User Input: None.
    • Validation: None.
  4. Apps
    • Purpose: Dedicated to quick downloads of apps.
    • Audience: Users looking for apps to try.
    • Content: App icons, small blurb, and download buttons.
    • Contains: Buttons.
    • Page Action: Buttons initiate download of an app.
    • User Input: None.
    • Validation: None.
  5. Contact
    • Purpose: A contact form to quickly send an inquiry directly to Steven.
    • Audience: Users looking to contact Steven.
    • Content: A form asking basic information and details of inquiry.
    • Contains: Dropdowns and buttons.
    • Page Action: Reset button clears the form. Submit button will show a "Request Sent" alert.
    • User Input: Text, email, textarea, dropdowns, check boxes.
    • Validation: Yes, ensure text areas are filled, email is in right format, and checkboxes are checked.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Globally: Most images will have a slight tilt animation when hovered on. Purely aesthetic reasons to help show where the cursor is. Inspiration: Just something I like to add on websites.
  • About Page: FAQ questions will act as drops downs that reveal the answer when clicked on. This will keep extra information hidden until activly searched for, reducing possibility of information overload to what the user sees at first glance. Inspiration: [Link]
  • Experience Page: Utilizes sections to keep all information on one page, but only shows one section at a time. These sections divide up space for each project. Inspiration: [Link]
  • Apps Page: Inclusion of download buttons for the applications Steven has made. Inspiration: App store app lists on mobile.
  • Contact Page: Having a clear/reset button and a submit button. Inspiration: [Link]
